## How to Push Content

### ▶ If you want to play **Videos Only**

1. **Upload Video Files**
   - Go to **Gallery**
   - Click **Upload Media**
   - Upload your video files

2. **Send Video to Device**
   - Navigate to **Manage Screen → Devices**
   - Select the device you want to update
   - Change **Content Type** to **Video**
   - Choose media files from the list
   - Click the **Action Button → Instant Update**

Your video will start playing immediately on the connected device.

---

### ▶ If you want to play **Images, Videos, or PDF**, use **Playlist Mode**

1. **Upload All Media to Gallery**
   - Go to **Gallery**
   - Upload images, videos, or PDF files you want to display

2. **Create Playlist**
   - Select multiple media files inside the gallery
   - Right-click → **Create Playlist**
   - Give a **Name** to your playlist and save it

3. **Push Playlist to Device**
   - Go to **Manage Screen → Devices**
   - Click on your device
   - Select **Playlist** instead of video content
   - Choose your playlist from the list
   - Click **Action Button → Instant Update**

Your screen will automatically start playing the selected playlist in sequence.

---

Once published, you can later modify the playlist, reorder files, or replace media anytime — the device will update instantly or based on your chosen schedule.
