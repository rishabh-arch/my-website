## How to Play **YouTube Playlist**

1. **Create a YouTube Playlist**
   - Open YouTube and sign in
   - Add videos to your playlist
   - Go to **Playlist Settings**
   - Set visibility to **Public** (or Unlisted if required)

2. **Copy Playlist Link**
   - Open the playlist on YouTube
   - Click **Share → Copy Link**

3. **Push to Device**
   - Go to **Manage Screen → Devices**
   - Select your device
   - Choose **YouTube** section (not *playlist* or *video* mode)
   - Paste the playlist URL
   - Click **Save / Instant Update**

Your device will now start streaming the YouTube playlist.

---

## How to Play **Website URL**

1. Copy the website link you want to display  
   (e.g., live dashboards, news sites, charts, menu boards, etc.)

2. Go to **Manage Screen → Devices**

3. Select your device and choose **Website Link** mode

4. Paste the website URL

5. Click **Instant Update** to push content immediately

Your screen will now display the website in full view.

---

You can switch between **YouTube**, **Website**, **Playlist**, or **Video** at any time from the device settings for flexible digital signage content playback.
