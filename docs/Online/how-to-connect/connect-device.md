## How to Connect Device ?

1. **Login to Bharat Signage**  
   Open your browser and sign in to your **Bharat Signage Online** account.

2. **Install Bharat Signage App on Device/TV**  
   Download the **Bharat Signage Player App** from the Playstore or the official website and install it on your Android TV or media device.

3. **Keep Wi-Fi/Internet ON**  
   Ensure your device has an active internet connection for verification and linking.

4. **Open the App to View Device Code**  
   Launch the Bharat Signage app on your TV.  
   A unique **Device ID / Code** will be displayed on screen.

5. **Add Device from Dashboard**  
   On the web dashboard navigate to:  
   **Manage Screen → Devices → Add Device**

6. **Enter Device Code & Connect**  
   Enter the Device ID shown on your TV and click **Connect**.

---

Once connected, your device will appear in the dashboard and will be ready to receive layouts, media, and schedules instantly.
